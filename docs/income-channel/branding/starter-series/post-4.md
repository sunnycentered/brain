📘 *Playbook Preview #1: Your First $1K/Month Affiliate Site in 30 Days*

Before the full playbook drops next week, here's the compressed version. If you implement these exact steps and commit 4 hours/day for a month, here's what happens:

---

## Week 1: Niche Selection & Validation (Days 1-7)

**Step 1 — Pick your niche:** Choose ONE of these criteria:
- Software/tools you actually use daily
- Topics where products cost $30+/month (higher affiliate commissions)
- Niches with at least 5 affiliate programs offering 20%+ recurring commissions
- NOT crypto, NOT gambling, NOT MLM — the gurus already flooded these

**My recommendation:** "AI tools for [specific profession]" — every day more professionals discover AI and need software recommendations. High intent, growing search volume, competitive but not saturated yet.

**Step 2 — Validate with free tools:**
- Google Trends → rising interest over past 12 months? ✓
- Ahrefs Free Plan → at least 50 keywords with 1K+ monthly searches? ✓
- Amazon Associates / partner programs → available for your top 3 product picks? ✓

---

## Week 2: Build & Content (Days 8-14)

**Step 3 — Set up the infrastructure:**
```
Platform: WordPress + GeneratePress (free) or Hugo (static site, $0 hosting on Cloudflare Pages)
Theme: Minimal. Speed > design. You're building a tool, not an art gallery.
Plugins: RankMath SEO (free), WP Rocket or Cloudflare CDN, Table of Contents Plus
Domain: Pick a name that includes your niche keyword (exact match still helps in 2026)
```

**Step 4 — Write your first 15 articles (5 days of work):**

Each article follows this exact structure:
1. Title format: "Best [Tool Category] for [Audience] in [Year]"
2. Hook paragraph: One sentence on why this category matters + what we'll cover
3. Comparison table: Top 5 products with pricing, key features, affiliate links
4. Individual reviews: 400-600 words per product, real pros/cons (be honest about downsides)
5. Verdict section: Clear recommendation based on use case + "Buy Now" CTA buttons

*Your first 15 articles take about 2 hours each to write thoroughly.* Total time investment: ~30 hours for the full batch.

---

## Week 3: Launch & Optimization (Days 15-21)

**Step 5 — Submit for indexing:**
- Google Search Console → submit sitemap immediately
- Bing Webmaster Tools → same process, free traffic from an overlooked source
- Submit top 3 articles to relevant subreddits (no affiliate links in the post, link in comments)

**Step 6 — Internal linking audit:** Every article must link to at least 3 other articles on your site. This creates the "content silo" Google rewards for topical authority.

---

## Week 4: Scale & Iterate (Days 22-30)

**Step 7 — Write 10 more articles** targeting longer-tail keywords (lower competition, faster to rank). Formula: "[Specific problem] + [tool name]" queries.

**Step 8 — Track everything:**
- Monthly page views per article (Google Analytics)
- Click-through rate on affiliate links
- Revenue per article (what actually earned vs. what took the most time)
- Double down on top performers, kill underperformers after 60 days

---
