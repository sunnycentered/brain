📘 *The Digital Nomad Income Stack — How 3 Streams Beat 1*

Most people I talk to about location independence have exactly ONE income stream. Usually freelancing or a remote job. That's not diversification — it's single-point failure with better WiFi.

Here's the framework I use and will be breaking down in weekly playbooks:

---

## Level 1: The Foundation (0-3 months to build)

*Time-to-first-dollar: 2-6 weeks*

**Affiliate content sites focused on software reviews**

Why this comes first:
- Low barrier to entry (you need a domain, hosting, and CMS)
- Compounds over time — old posts still earn years later
- No audience required upfront (SEO does the distribution)
- Scales linearly with content volume

My approach: Pick ONE niche you understand well. Build 30-50 high-quality review articles targeting "best [tool] for [use case]" keywords. Average 2,000 words per article. Internal link everything to your cornerstone piece.

*Result from my first site:* $1,200/month by month 4. Took 6 weeks to build the initial content.

---

## Level 2: The Accelerator (3-6 months)

*Time-to-first-dollar: 1-3 months after foundation is live*

**AI-powered micro-SaaS tools**

Why this comes second:
- Recurring revenue (monthly subscriptions)
- High margins once built (~90% gross margin)
- Can be productized and eventually automated away from you

My approach: Look at your top 3 most-requested features from Level 1 readers. Build a focused tool that solves ONE problem better than existing options. Price it $9-29/month. Market through your content site's audience + Hacker News / Product Hunt launches.

*Result from my portfolio:* Peak $12K/month across 4 tools before consolidating to the one survivor ($8K/month).

---

## Level 3: The Multiplier (6-12 months)

*Time-to-first-dollar: 3-6 months after accelerator starts*

**Automated income funnels**

Why this comes last:
- Requires established audience/trust from Levels 1 & 2
- Higher technical complexity
- Maintenance burden that compounds without systems

My approach: Productize your knowledge into a digital course, template pack, or SaaS add-on. Drive traffic through your existing content + email list. Automate the sales process with an embedded checkout and delivery system.
