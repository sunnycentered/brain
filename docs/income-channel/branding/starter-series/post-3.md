🔥 *The "Digital Nomad Guru" Industry Is Broken — Here's What Actually Works*

Let me be clear: I'm not going to sell you a course. I'm not going to charge you $2,000 for a PDF of things you can find free on YouTube. And I'm definitely not going to post fake income screenshots with stock photos of beaches and Lambos.

The gurus have taught us what *doesn't* work:

❌ **The "I'll show you how I made $50K/month dropshipping" story** — Can't find the person behind the posts. Probably a bot account. If they're really making that from e-commerce, they're on a yacht, not running Facebook ads for $5 landing pages.

❌ **The "passive income while you sleep" promise** — Nothing is passive at launch. Anything becomes "passive" only after hundreds of hours of active building. Calling it passive isn't honesty — it's marketing manipulation.

❌ **The "quit your job tomorrow and travel forever" timeline** — The people who actually do this successfully are either pre-saved with 6+ months of runway or had a business before the Instagram post about it. Both paths work but neither is as easy as the Reel makes it look.

—

*What @DigitalNomadTech Does Differently:*

✅ **I show the failures.** My AI wrapper that tanked after OpenAI changed their pricing? I'll write about it. The affiliate site that never broke $500/month despite 3 months of work? Also covered. You learn more from dead projects than from curated wins.

✅ **I show raw numbers with timestamps.** Not "screenshot at 2 AM my third vacation day" photos. Actual Stripe dashboards with date ranges, server costs deducted, and tax estimates included. Real math, not aspirational math.

✅ **The playbooks are free because the content IS the marketing.** If you implement what I teach here and make money from it, that's proof the channel works. I'll earn from the tools and services we review — naturally aligned incentives. No course upsells required.

✅ **Community over audience.** We're building this as a group of builders helping each other, not followers being funneled into something. Your feedback shapes what gets published here. If a tool or strategy interests you — mention it in DMs and I'll review it.

—

*I started this channel because I was tired of watching well-meaning people get burned by income gurus. The path to location independence exists. It's just not as sexy as the alternative.*

It looks like spreadsheets, not sunset photos.
