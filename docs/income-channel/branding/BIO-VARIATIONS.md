## Hashtag Strategy

Telegram bios support hashtags. Recommended approach:

- **Include 2-3 max** in the bio — enough for search visibility without cluttering readability
- **Prioritize:** `#RemoteWork`, `#PassiveIncome`, `#DigitalNomad` (highest-volume tags)
- **Optional fourth:** `#TechTools` if space permits — it's unique to your channel

The options above that include hashtags stay within the 160-char limit. If you remove hashtags, all bios become shorter and leave breathing room for future edits.
